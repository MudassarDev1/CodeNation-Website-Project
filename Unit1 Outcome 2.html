Review

2.1 / 2.2 Review the ongoing use of IT tools and change approach as needed – in making your own website, are you happy with the tools you used? E.g. Visual Studio Code, Trello

In making my own website the tools I used were Visual Studio Code as a text editor for HTMl and CSS, and Trello to help me manage my project and keep track of my work; what's needed and what has been done. Using GitHub helped me instantly view any changes I made to my project.
I am very happy with the tools I used such as Visual Studio Code and Trello as they helped me to complete my project but also as I understood what they were used for.

2.3 Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project – are you happy with your website?

I am happy with my website as it does what I intended it to do. I have inserted a navigation tool bar at the top and bottom of each page for easy navigation around the site. I have used different fonts and colours to identify different sections in pages and also have inserted some images to brighten the pages up.
As this is the first website I have created I am happy with the overall look of the site although with further research into and knowledge of HTML and CSS I would try to make it a little bit more professional looking and easier on the eye.

2.4 Describe further improvements you can make to your project

Having reviewed my project/website I feel I could change the background images on the pages as currently there seems to be too much going on in the background and in places it is a little difficult to read the written content.
I was also intending to insert a video somewhere on one of the pages as opposed to just images.
Another further improvement I could make is to make the website accessible on mobile and tablet devices.
I also think that I could have created a new email address to insert into the ‘contact us’ section of the website.

2.5 Review outcomes to make sure they match requirements and are fit for purpose

Having looked at the final website I feel it looks like what I intended it to look like. The navigation toolbars at the top and bottom of each page help users to navigate around the site easily. It includes a section about me and breaks the different sections of the assignments into separate pages. I feel the website is fit for purpose for what it was intended to do.
